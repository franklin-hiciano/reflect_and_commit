Reflect & Commit
The app runs on one thesis: you'll execute your own ideas if you keep thinking of them, no matter how crazy or life-changing they may be. Asking good questions is an extraordinary way of generating life-changing ideas. Thus, use questions to identify can do tomorrow to change your life a bit. I.e. prompt yourself into thinking of solutions to your major problems before bed. Just write questions that will improve your life, that's all.
