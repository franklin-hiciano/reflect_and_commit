Reflect & Commit
Asking yourself questions before bed is very powerful. You can use it to think of solutions to your major problems before tomorrow. You can ask yourself to envision 10 years down the line, and have enough clarity to make life-altering decisions the next day. Or work harder than ever before.
In this app, you will write yourself questions, then it will ask you them. For all the ideas you have for changing your life, it will hold you accountable for doing them. But take initiative yourself! Most ideas from this app that have changed my life, I got up and did it right after writing it. It's the ideas where I waited until the commit screen that never came to fruition.
